Full Biography

Brandon Tyler Feakins, professionally known as Brandon Skyler, is an accomplished multifaceted artist born on June 26, 1990. His career spans both music and acting, showcasing exceptional versatility and unwavering dedication to his craft.

Acting Career: Brandon has delivered critically acclaimed performances in prestigious films including Mapplethorpe and Vice (2018), Midway (2019), and more recently It Ends with Us (2024). His television work, particularly his role as Spencer Dutton in the acclaimed series 1923, has solidified his reputation as a nuanced and compelling performer capable of complex character development.

Musical Journey: As a sophisticated singer-songwriter influenced by legends such as Michael Jackson, Dolly Parton, and Mariah Carey, Brandon has authored over 200 original compositions. His music represents a refined fusion of African rhythms with contemporary production techniques, creating a distinctive sonic identity that resonates with international audiences.

Artistic Vision: Brandon is committed to artistic authenticity and meaningful creative expression. Whether performing on stage, creating in the studio, or acting on screen, he brings uncompromising passion and professionalism to every endeavor. His objective is not merely to entertain but to inspire and forge lasting connections with audiences worldwide.
