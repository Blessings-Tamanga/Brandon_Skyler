Simple Guide (For First-Time Users)

  1. Click a tab: Filmography, Acting, Gallery, Team, or Messages.
  2. Click Add to create a new item.
  3. Fill the form fields and click Save.
  4. Use Edit to update an item or Delete to remove it.
  5. If the public page is open in another tab, it updates automatically after save/delete.
