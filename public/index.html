Full Biography

Brandon Tyler Feakins, known professionally as Brandon Sklenar, was born on June 26, 1990, in Dover, New Jersey, U.S. Raised in Northern New Jersey, he is the son of Francine Sklenar and Bruce Feakins.

He graduated from Hackettstown High School (2008), briefly attended County College of Morris for exercise science, moved to California after two months, then relocated to Los Angeles, where he worked odd jobs and was signed by a manager at age 20.

Early Career (2011-2017): Film debut in Cornered (2011), TV debut in Dating Rules from My Future Self (2012), with appearances in Chance (2014), Truth Be Told (2015), Hunky Dory (2016), Temple (2017), and New Girl (2017).

Breakthrough (2018): Portrayed Edward Mapplethorpe in Mapplethorpe (screened at Tribeca Film Festival) and appeared in Vice alongside Amy Adams, Christian Bale, Steve Carell, and Sam Rockwell.

Later Roles: Midway (2019) as George H. Gay Jr., The Big Ugly (2020), Karen (2021), Emily the Criminal (2022), Futra Days (2022), It Ends with Us (2024) as Atlas Corrigan, Drop (2025), and The Housemaid (2025) as Andrew Winchester.

Award: Best Actor, Vienna Independent Film Festival (2022) for Futra Days.
